Flex-Direction

Properties

The flex-direction property specifies the direction of the flexible items. It has four possible values:

  • row (default): The flexible items are displayed in a row, from left to right.
  • row-reverse: The flexible items are displayed in a row, but in reverse order, from right to left.
  • column: The flexible items are displayed in a column, from top to bottom.
  • column-reverse: The flexible items are displayed in a column, but in reverse order, from bottom to top.
